From this website one could request for blood in any specific location and contact with the donor very easily. The main aim is to provide fast and efficient way to gain attention of potential donors in the need of hour.

The goals and objectives:

1. To allow the probable recipients to make search and match the donors, and make request for the blood.
2. To provide an efficient donor and blood stock management functions to the blood bank by recording the donor and blood details.
3. To provide synchronized and centralized donor and blood stock database.
4. To provide immediate storage and retrieval of data and information.
5. To track the donor's current address.
6. To allow recipients to make request for blood through SMS and Internet
